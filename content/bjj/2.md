On the drive to each class, the anxiousness is slowly going away. I feel this is 
because I started to know what to expect. All the classes have the same layout
as explained above, learning a new technique each class. Whether that be a
submission or a position to get more leverage over your opponent.

I've decided each class I aim to atleast learn one new thing, doesn't strictly 
need to be the technique taught by the instructors. 

For this class I learnt to reserve my energy while rolling, I was
releasing all of my energy straight away, the opponent would lock me in a 
position and just let me wiggle and squirm until I got tired, leading to an
easy sub.

I just need to relax while rolling, take my time and put thought to what I do, 
it's my second class so I know that's pretty difficult given I don't really 
know what I'm doing yet.
