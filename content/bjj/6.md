# What We Trained

We practised the ankle pick takedown, americana submission, and common escapes
and ways to deal with those escapes for both.

# What I Learnt

Since my last class, I expected to be put into a full guard again, so I
researched some ways of getting out of that. As expected I was put into that
position and got out of it. I also learnt some different types
of submissions from a training partner which were handy, can't remember what it 
they were called. I also went from side-control to full mount at one point, I
still got my ass beat but that was pretty cool.

# What to Improve

One of my sparing partners put me into the same front headlock at least three
times, so I need to look at some ways of getting out of that, but I also need
to try and change up my approach a bit if the opponent is consistantly putting
me in the same position. I think it's just because the only takedown I know is
the ankle pick learnt in this class, but still going to keep note of it.

# How I Felt After

100% better compared to my last class, I think doing research after my class
helped heaps. I accomplished my goal of getting out of full guard which I think
boosted my confidence heaps.
