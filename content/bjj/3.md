It had been a week since I did my last class, I could feel that I kinda went
back in what I had learnt, maybe I had harder opponents? Not too sure, but was
still fun. I rolled with someone much larger than me this time and while I've
heard people smaller than thier opponent can still easily get a sub, that was
definitly not the case in this instance, it's definitly the lack of knowledge
I have at the moment.

The last opponent I spared with was super cool, we had a chat about things to 
know as a beginner, and what it's going to be like for the next year or so. He 
basically told me for the first year I will be getting submitted 98% of the
time, I expected this to demotivate me but I feel like it has only given me
more motivation.
